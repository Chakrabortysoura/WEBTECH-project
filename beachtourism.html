Let's Explore !
West Bengal’s coastline along the Bay of Bengal is adorned with a variety of beaches, each offering a unique experience to visitors. The state’s beaches are known for their natural beauty, ranging from bustling shores with local markets to tranquil retreats away from the crowds.Visitors can immerse themselves in the natural splendor of golden beaches, witness the cultural tapestry through local cuisine and markets, and engage in various recreational activities. Easily accessible and rich in scenic beauty, these beaches serve as perfect retreats for those seeking a blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural experiences along the Bay of Bengal.
Here is the list of the 5 most beautiful beaches in the West Bengal :
Digha
Tajpur
Bakkhali
Mandarmani
Shankarpur


1.DIGHA






Digha Beach is the most visited beach in Bengal. And it is popular among tourists, newlyweds as well as residents. This beach is classified into two parts, namely, Old Digha Beach and New Digha Beach. The New Digha Beach is especially for couples or newlyweds who want some privacy.
Families must visit the Old Digha. This beach shifts its course in day and night time, which makes it more famous. During the day, the tides rise towards land, and at night it goes away from the ground.

The most suitable time to tour this beach is from October to February.

What’s Special: Digha Beach is known for its tranquil environment and peaceful vibe, making it a perfect destination for a relaxing getaway.
Things to Do: Enjoy a boat ride to Jambu Dwip or visit the Fraseganj Fishing Harbour to experience the local culture and lifestyle.
Where is it: Located only 122 kilometers from Kolkata, Digha Beach is in the South 24 Parganas District of West Bengal.
How to Reach: With the newly constructed bridge, reaching Digha Beach by road transport has become easier. Earlier, one had to take a ferry to reach this island beach.
Nearby Best Resorts to Stay: Hotel Amarabati and Bakhali Tourist Lodge are some of the nearby resorts offering comfortable accommodation options.
Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Digha Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities.
Nearby Places to See: Explore nearby attractions like Fraseganj, Jambu Dwip, Henry Island, and the Bishalakkhi Temple to experience the rich culture and beauty of the region.







2.TAJPUR








This beach is as usual for a long time; it is the untouched beach of Bengal. It is also called the Virgin beach of West Bengal as it remained unexplored and unchanged for a long time. The exquisite sunrise and sunset of the Tajpur beach with background music of waves and breeze must be experienced once in life.
It is heaven for those who want a vacation from the rushed and busy city life as it is calm, quiet, and untouched by humans. The most suitable time to tour this beach is from October to February.
What’s Special: Tajpur Beach is known for its pristine beauty and peaceful atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for a relaxing holiday.
Things to Do: Indulge in beach activities like swimming, sunbathing, and beach volleyball. You can also take a stroll along the shore and enjoy the scenic views.
Where is it: Tajpur Beach is located in the Purba Medinipur district of West Bengal, approximately 170 kilometers from Kolkata.
How to Reach: The beach can be reached by road from Kolkata via NH 16. It is also accessible by train, with the nearest railway station being Contai.
Nearby Best Resorts to Stay: There are several resorts and guesthouses near Tajpur Beach that offer comfortable accommodation options for tourists.
Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Tajpur Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities.
Nearby Places to See: Explore nearby attractions like Mandarmani Beach, Shankarpur Beach, and Digha Beach for a complete coastal experience.







3.BAKKHALI








In West Bengal, it is famous among newlyweds for honeymoons and for couples to spend some quality time together. It has luxurious villas and resorts too for offering full privacy to the love birds. Mandarmani Beach went under commercialization recently, and hence water sports are famous here.
The exact, beautiful blue color of the beach, exquisite sunset, and sunrise is the perfect scenery to experience with your beloved. The best time to tour this beach is from October to May.
What’s Special: Mandarmani Beach is known for its long stretch of pristine sandy beach and tranquil atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for a peaceful getaway.
Things to Do: Indulge in beach activities like swimming, sunbathing, and beach volleyball. You can also enjoy a relaxing beach walk and soak in the scenic views.
Where is it: Mandarmani Beach is located in the East Midnapore district of West Bengal, approximately 180 kilometers from Kolkata.
How to Reach: The beach can be reached by road from Kolkata via NH 116B. It is also accessible by train, with the nearest railway station being Contai.
Nearby Best Resorts to Stay: There are several resorts and guesthouses near Mandarmani Beach that offer comfortable accommodation options for tourists.
Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Mandarmani Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities.
Nearby Places to See: Explore nearby attractions like Tajpur Beach, Shankarpur Beach, and Digha Beach for a complete coastal experience.







4.MANDARMANI








Bakkhali Beach is the beach that overlooks the Bay of Bengal. And unlike other beaches, it is far away from commercialization. It is one of the quietest, most serene, and less visited beaches. Near the beach, there are some private properties, offering the property owners a part of the sea for enjoying sunset, and sunrise and hearing the musical sound of breeze and waves.
As it is the less visited beach, it is untouched by humans and hence in its pure and raw form, which makes it more attractive for tourists who want to see nature in its original form. The best time to tour this beach is from October to March.
What’s Special: Bakkhali Beach is known for its serene environment and pristine beauty, making it a perfect destination for a peaceful getaway.
Things to Do: Enjoy a relaxing beach walk, indulge in water sports, or simply soak in the tranquil atmosphere of the beach.
Where is it: Bakkhali Beach is located in the South 24 Parganas district of West Bengal, approximately 125 kilometers from Kolkata.
How to Reach: The beach can be reached by road from Kolkata via NH 12. It is also accessible by train, with the nearest railway station being Namkhana.
Nearby Best Resorts to Stay: There are several resorts and guesthouses near Bakkhali Beach that offer comfortable accommodation options for tourists.
Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Bakkhali Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities.
Nearby Places to See: Explore nearby attractions like Fraserganj Beach, Henry Island, and the Bishalakkhi Temple for a complete coastal experience.







5.SHANKARPUR








Shankarpur beach is also one of the unexplored and untouched beaches of West Bengal. If you want to experience something like the old world, you must visit Shankarpur beach. In West Bengal, it is in the East Medinipur district. This beach is near Digha Beach, approximately 10 km away. It is famous for its soothing and calm environment.
Shankarpur beach has undergone development recently. Luxurious beaches are made for visitors. This beach is renowned for fishing and activities related to that. The best time to tour this beach is from December to April.
What’s Special: Shankarpur Beach is known for its serene environment and pristine beauty, making it an ideal destination for a peaceful getaway.

Things to Do: Enjoy a relaxing beach walk, indulge in water sports, or simply soak in the tranquil atmosphere of the beach.
Where is it: Shankarpur Beach is located in the East Midnapore district of West Bengal, approximately 185 kilometers from Kolkata.
How to Reach: The beach can be reached by road from Kolkata via NH 116B. It is also accessible by train, with the nearest railway station being Contai.
Nearby Best Resorts to Stay: There are several resorts and guesthouses near Shankarpur Beach that offer comfortable accommodation options for tourists.
Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Shankarpur Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities.
Nearby Places to See: Explore nearby attractions like Tajpur Beach, Mandarmani Beach, and Digha Beach for a complete coastal experience.
